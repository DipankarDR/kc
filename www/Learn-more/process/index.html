The Streamlined Campaign Building Process

Campaign creation is hassle free when you have us as your guide. A few simple steps help you to create the campaign page efficiently. Six effective steps to create your campaign page:

1Signup to get started with the campaign

Sign up and get started with a campaign of your choice. Your cause is as important to us as it is to you. Wait no longer and connect with us through Facebook or login with Google and make your way to the best campaign in town.

2Landing first time needs to create a campaign

Thank you for registering with us. It is a pleasure to have you onboard. As it is your first time with us, you need to create a campaign. Fill in all the necessary details and your campaign is ready to go LIVE. Create the campaign and see the love pouring in.

3 Easy self editeable campaign buildup

Fundraising Summary

It is important to keep it short, simple and sweet. This page is only going to be seen when people visit the crowd funding page. This is especially true when you share it on the social media. It is imperative to get to the core of the reason and state why the cause matters to you.

Goal

When you set a goal, it creates an impetus while generating a target that one can easily aim for. It is vital to be ambitious, while at the same time be practical.

It is crucial that you let the crowd know why you care enough to create the campaign hence it is important to get the storyboard correct as it should appeal to your donors. State the valid reasons for the unique bond that you share with the cause.

Image

An image is worth a thousand words. Captured moments help define a story in an effective manner. It is vital that you highlight the faces of your community or supporters who are likely to benefit from your hard work.

The what, when, how and when

Content is of utmost priority that helps you to win over the pledges. This further has a direct relationship to the amount of money you raise. It is extremely vital that you communicate directly with the audience at large and reach out to them.

Updates

Updates might seem insignificant but they are extremely important. You need to keep your supporters updated about the latest happenings. You can state the present happenings or your future aim. You can as well simply thank your supporters for the support they have put in. An engaged audience is more likely to spread the word effectively. More you share and update utilization of funds, more likely you attract new donors hence do regularly update and let existing and potential donors know how and when funds are being used.

4Complete the Profile

Tell us something more about yourself and let your donors know more about you. Remember to add a profile picture, as your supporters would want to have a face with a name and let your donors know you better.

5 Manage Campaigns through Dashboard

Congratulations on creating your first effective fundraising campaign. It is time to manage your campaign well and see it through to achieving your funding goals. Here you have your personal dashboard where you are able to manage your campaign more effectively. Here you are able to see total donations you have received till date, social media sharing report, creates a new campaign, view all published campaign.

6Manage Visitors Questions & Infos

Be the spokesperson of your campaign. It is important to clarify every doubt that crosses the minds of the visitors. In this way, you can manage all information and questions of the visitors efficiently. Through your dashboard you able to reply to each query your donors may have before donating to your cause so do look out for such queries and reply to each as quickly as possible. More promptly you reply, more confidence your donors will have on you.
