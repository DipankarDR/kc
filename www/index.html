How it Works!

Create & Publish Own Campaign

Create your own campaign for which you require funds. State the amount of fund required by you as the fund goals to be fulfilled. It is also imperative to state the number of days that are available to gather all the funds.

Spread the word

Share your campaign across all social networking platforms like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and others. Share it with anyone and everyone you know through word of mouth and make sure your campaign spreads like wildfire.

Get Funded

We accept donations in all forms and hence there is no stopping of funds from coming in. Watch the power a crowd holds and feel the love.
