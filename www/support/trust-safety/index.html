Trust and Safety

Safety is our first and foremost priority at Krowdcircle.com. Our aim is to make the entire process of fundraising a cakewalk for you. We give maximum importance to sincerity and honesty and hence work accordingly.

Important things you need to know about us:

We have a strong team of charity partners who need to go through an age-old process of verification and authentication. This process calls for submission of valid important documents. We verify the credibility of each charity prior its account activation on Krowdcircle.com. Additionally, we also perform necessary verification for the team that raises funds for individual needs. After thorough verification, we only approve of the authentic ones. We at Krowdcircle do not encourage the donation pages, which attempt to avoid the prescribed methods of donations. People are directed to a large button of ‘Contribute Now’, which is found on every donation page of Krowdcircle. You can report any page, which requests for direct payments. You can also report against any individual or user who contacts you for arranging an alternative method of payment.
Be very careful about revealing any personal information that includes address details, contact number. We would like you to know that we do not contact users via this method. You can share the email address when needed. You need to be cautious in instances when you receive offers from any third party fundraising company or from any individual offering personal assistance. Do not ever entertain anyone personally contacting you through the Krowdcircle page. Any kind of suspicious activity need to be directly reported to us at the earliest.

Reasons why a fundraiser need not worry:

How can one trust the process of payment?

We have extended our partnership with trustable payment gateways like Paypal. We also accept all modes of online payment that includes debit card, net banking and credit card. Facilities like offline modes of payment such as cheque pick up and cash are provided.

Is it safe?

We ensure that all payments are secured. The online payments of the donors are kept safe. Additionally, your money is also secured until you get ready to withdraw the money through electronic bank transfer. We keep all banking details safely with us.

Do I owe any amount to Krowdcircle?

While it is completely free to share and create the online fundraising campaign, krowdcircle deducts a certain amount of fee from each of the donation you receive. This includes the service tax as well as the payment gateway charges. The fee is deducted automatically and thus you no longer need to worry about paying us any money.

How are my donors informed about my intention?

Krowdcircle being an open platform encourages transparency in every single way. Regular updates can be put up on the campaign page that keeps the backers informed.

Reasons why a donor need not worry:

How to know whether the campaigner is authentic or not?

Even after continuous controlling and monitoring, it is difficult for krowdcircle.com to guarantee true and precise information. It is advisable for the donors to make a payment only when they personally support and know the cause of the campaigners. The donors need to be completely aware of the cause before contributing.

Whom do I ask questions about a campaign?

We maintain complete honesty as our foremost policy. So if you have any questions, you can simply let us know. We will give valuable suggestions and make sure that we directly connect you with campaign owner.

Will the website be sharing my personal details?

Each detail that you submit is kept completely confidential. We ensure that all unauthorized access is avoided by us. You no longer need to worry about the privacy of the information.

Does the donation reach the cause?

The money donated directly reaches the bank account that is associated with the campaign of the fundraiser.

Where do I contact for more questions?

If you have any additional questions, then email us at info@krowdcircle.com.
