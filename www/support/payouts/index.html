Payouts

Your funds cannot be granted while the campaign is going on. The funds are to be transferred to your account only at the end of the campaign. Irrespective of the target, the entire fund amount collected is transferred to the designated account.

So when do I acquire the funds post the campaign?

We make the payouts post 14 days of the campaign. It is imperative to note that Krowdcircle.com does not make any payouts to ongoing campaigns. With the campaign ending, the funds automatically are transferred in a 2 weeks time to the bank account. This cycle starts only from the day the campaign ends and all necessary documents have been uploaded. For example, if the campaign ends on 1 December and the necessary documents are uploaded on 7 December, then the payout is given on 21 December (2 weeks). This makes way for an extremely smooth and fast payout. For any inquiry, you can write to us at payouts@Krowdcircle.com.

How much of the funds collected is going to be credited to my account?

A flat fee of 5% + VAT is charged from the funds collected. The amount available post the deduction is transferred to the bank account.

Define Swift Code and Intermediary Bank details and how can I find them?

Sort Code:

This code is required to transfer amount in the foreign currency, as each bank comprises of unique sort code.

Intermediary bank details:

This bank is a third party that has been used by your bank to enable settlement of funds and international transfer. These are required for transferring GBP (foreign funds) collected in the campaign. The forex department of your bank will provide this information.

Can I get information about the people donating?

Yes, you can get the information. You need to log on to your account at the campaign page. There is a download file option located on the top right-hand side, which comprises of all details related to donors. Get in touch with info@Krowdcircle.com for any queries.
